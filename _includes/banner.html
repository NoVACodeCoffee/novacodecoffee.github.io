NoVa Code & Coffee

This group meets every other week on Saturdays either at Custom Ink (map) headquarters in Fairfax VA, or Excella Consulting (map) headquarters Court House VA. Please see our meetup page to confirm the location. Developers of all skill levels come together to work on and discuss projects with each other. You may also be interested in our sister groups DC Code & Coffee and Alexandria Code & Coffee.
